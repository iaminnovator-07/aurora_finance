# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

# Aurora TIA

## AI-Powered Enterprise Finance Operations Platform

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

Aurora TIA is an Enterprise AI Finance Operations Platform designed to automate the complete Accounts Payable lifecycle—from vendor email ingestion to ERP-ready invoice processing.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, OCR, Explainable AI, Business Rule Automation, Trust Intelligence, and Finance Analytics into a unified workflow that significantly reduces manual effort, accelerates processing, improves compliance, and enhances financial visibility.

Rather than replacing finance professionals, Aurora TIA augments their capabilities by automating repetitive operational work while allowing teams to focus on financial decision-making and exception handling.

# AI Cost Optimization

Aurora TIA is designed with enterprise-scale AI efficiency.

Instead of repeatedly calling AI services throughout the workflow, the platform follows a **Process Once – Reuse Everywhere** architecture.

### Optimization Strategies

* One OCR execution per document
* One AI inference per invoice
* Structured JSON reused across all downstream modules
* Intelligent OCR caching
* AI response caching
* Duplicate document fingerprinting
* Incremental Gmail synchronization
* Confidence-based AI routing
* Local validation before invoking AI
* Smart exception processing

Expected Benefits

* Reduced AI inference costs
* Lower API consumption
* Faster response times
* Improved scalability
* Better infrastructure efficiency
